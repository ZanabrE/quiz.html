THE OUTBREAK DISEASE CORONAVIRUS (COVID-19)

There is an outbreak that is affecting our health and no many people know about this disease the symptoms and how deadly could be for humans this deadly disease its called CORONAVIRUS (COVID-19) and spread across the globe and still spreading some people don't have an idea how dangerous this virus is and its symptoms which is different than a regular cold, or flu. At work are several patients concerned about this disease afraid that their medications could be contaminate or asking about our supply which we have a team that actively monitors very close with the current international and domestic environment for COVID-19 with the help of FDA (Food & Drug Administration) that also is monitoring closely. Here are the symptoms of the cold, which some of the symptoms are: Sneezing, Stuffy or runny nose, Sore throat, Coughing, Post-nasal drip, Watery eyes, and Fever (which is rare on most people). Which cold symptoms last for up to 10 days to 14 days after that period we get better. In the other hand Coronavirus symptoms are: Fever, Cough, Shortness of breath which are severe some people have mild symptoms, which are: Sore throat and runny nose, some of them Diarrhea, Nausea, and vomiting. The sever symptoms will kill people after 14 days. It is recommend that you wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, do not touch your eyes, nose or mouth with unwashed hands, avoid close contact with people who are sick, stay home if you are sick, and disinfect frequently after in contact with objects and surfaces.
